To Log a New Workout:

Click the “Workout Log” tab Select the date of your workout you want to enter in the date box under “Filter workout by date:” (note, if you have completed another workout on this date, that workout will show in the log after you select the date) Click the “Start New Workout” button Enter each set with the information below in the respective text box: Exercise (currently, only the following exercises can be logged on Gains Task; please be sure to use these precise terms, otherwise your set will not be logged!) Bench Press Push Ups Dumbbell Row Bicep Curl Squat Leg Press Reps (if doing weights) or Time (if doing cardio) Weight (if doing weights) Rating (a description or emoji of how the set went) Click the “Add Row+” button Repeat steps 4 through 6 for as many sets as you can handle, athlete! When done, you can close the browser window OR review your workout logs or performance history. You do not need to do anything else to save your workout.

To Review a Workout Log:

Click the “Workout Log” tab Select the date of the workout log you want to review in the date box under “Filter workout by date.” All workouts logged for this date will populate in a chart for your review. If you wish to review another workout log, simply select the date of the other workout in the date box under “Filter workout by date.”

To Review Performance History for a Specific Exercise:

Click the “Performance History” tab In the “Exercise Type” text box, enter the exercise whose performance history you wish to review (please be sure to use these precise terms, otherwise you will not be able to submit the form and review your performance history!) Bench Press Push Ups Dumbbell Row Bicep Curl Squat Leg Press Click the “Submit” button All sets you have ever performed of the exercise you requested will populate in a chart for your review If you wish to review performance history of a different exercise, simply repeat steps 2 and 3 as often as you wish.
